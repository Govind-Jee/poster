Key Findings

  • The stock shows a consistent long-term upward trend, with a sharp increase from 2016 to 2017.
  • The 20-day and 50-day EMAs are effective indicators for tracking the price trend direction.
  • The LSTM model successfully captures the general trend, though with some deviation in daily volatility.
  • The stock shows high volatility with large price swings.
